OpenSpace US

About

To all biohackers and diy makers! We have made for you a unified and updated website with all current biolabs, workshops, fablabs, and hakerspaces in the US. Feel free to reach out to us if we have missed one. Our recent addition is the support a makerspace page. We want to help new makerspaces grow by adding a page dedicated on sharing their goal, missions, and where to fund them. If you are currently working on developing a makerspace, please contact us with your location, purpose, and contact information, and we will get in touch with you in less than 2 business days.
